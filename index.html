Your birthday letter

Hey Tannu 💛 — Happy Birthday to the prettiest, cutest girl I know. I don't even know where to start, because there's honestly so much I want to say to you… and somehow words still don't feel enough.

You're that one person in my life with whom I can share literally anything in the world. No filters, no hesitation… just me being me. And somehow, with you, even time feels different — like it runs too fast, and I'm always there thinking, "just one more minute."

You've done so much for me, more than you even realize. From improving my fashion sense to fixing my vocabulary (yes… hai to haan 😂 — I know I've messed that up more than a thousand times), you've changed me completely as a person. You've taught me how to be gentle, how to understand people better, and how to grow.

You've always motivated me, always stood by me. And even when you felt like you were troubling me — doing things for you never felt like a burden. It always made me genuinely happy.

I know things weren't always perfect between us. There were times when I messed up… probably more than I should have. But you still chose to stay. You gave me chances, again and again, when I didn't even deserve them. And that's something I'll never forget.

You are, without a doubt, the best person in my life. And I know you always will be. You understand me in a way no one else does… and that means everything to me.

I'm really sorry that I'm not there with you on your birthday. I wish I could be there, celebrate it with you, and make you smile in person. But I promise — this isn't it. I'm going to surprise you soon, and we'll celebrate properly. 🎉

Till then… just know — You're incredibly special to me. More than I say, more than I show. And yeah… you'll always be my favorite person 💛

Happy Birthday, Tannu. 🌸
